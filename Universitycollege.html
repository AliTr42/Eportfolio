University College of Louvain at Hainaut

I have already done two years at the University College of Louvain. I developed so much knowledge quickly during those two years. it was quite tough, the level gap between the higher school and the university college was enormous. And it was even more difficult for me because I had started from zero, I had no knowledge about computers science.
